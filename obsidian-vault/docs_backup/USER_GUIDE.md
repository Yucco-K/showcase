## Getting Started

### 1.1 Account Creation

#### For New Users

1. **Navigate to the Login Page**

   - Click the "Login" button in the top navigation
   - Select "Create Account" option

2. **Complete Registration**

   - Enter your email address
   - Create a secure password (minimum 8 characters)
   - Click "Sign Up"

3. **Email Verification**
   - Check your email for verification link
   - Click the link to activate your account
   - Return to the application and log in

#### For Existing Users

1. **Login Process**

   - Click "Login" in the navigation
   - Enter your email and password
   - Click "Sign In"

2. **Password Recovery**
   - Click "Forgot Password?" on login page
   - Enter your email address
   - Follow the reset instructions in your email

### 1.2 Profile Setup

#### Personal Information

1. **Access Profile Settings**

   - Click your avatar in the top navigation
   - Select "My Page" from the dropdown

2. **Update Profile**

   - **Full Name**: Enter your display name
   - **Biography**: Add a brief description about yourself
   - **Avatar**: Upload a profile picture (JPEG, PNG, GIF, WebP up to 5MB)

3. **Save Changes**
   - Click "Update Profile" to save your information

#### Avatar Management

- **Upload**: Click the camera icon on your avatar
- **Delete**: Hover over avatar and click the trash icon
- **Supported Formats**: JPEG, PNG, GIF, WebP
- **Size Limit**: Maximum 5MB

## Troubleshooting

### 6.1 Common Issues

#### Login Problems

1. **Forgot Password**

   - Click "Forgot Password?" on login page
   - Enter registered email address
   - Check email for reset instructions
   - Create new password

2. **Account Locked**

   - Multiple failed login attempts
   - Wait 15 minutes before retrying
   - Contact support if issue persists

3. **Email Verification**
   - Check spam/junk folder
   - Click verification link in email
   - Request new verification if expired

#### Payment Issues

1. **Payment Declined**

   - Verify card information
   - Check available balance
   - Contact bank for authorization
   - Try alternative payment method

2. **Duplicate Charges**

   - Check bank statement for actual charges
   - Contact support with transaction details
   - Provide order confirmation emails

3. **Missing Downloads**
   - Check email for download links
   - Verify email address is correct
   - Contact support with order number

#### Technical Problems

1. **Page Loading Issues**

   - Clear browser cache and cookies
   - Try different browser
   - Check internet connection
   - Disable browser extensions

2. **Mobile Compatibility**

   - Update mobile browser
   - Enable JavaScript
   - Check device storage space
   - Restart mobile device

3. **Performance Issues**
   - Close unnecessary browser tabs
   - Check internet speed
   - Clear browser history
   - Update browser to latest version

### 6.2 Error Messages

#### Common Error Codes

1. **404 - Page Not Found**

   - Check URL spelling
   - Navigate from homepage
   - Contact support if persistent

2. **500 - Server Error**

   - Refresh page
   - Try again in few minutes
   - Contact support if recurring

3. **403 - Access Denied**
   - Verify login status
   - Check account permissions
   - Contact admin for access

#### Browser Compatibility

1. **Supported Browsers**

   - **Chrome**: Version 90+
   - **Firefox**: Version 88+
   - **Safari**: Version 14+
   - **Edge**: Version 90+

2. **Mobile Browsers**
   - **iOS Safari**: iOS 14+
   - **Chrome Mobile**: Version 90+
   - **Samsung Internet**: Version 14+

## FAQ

### 7.1 General Questions

#### Account Management

**Q: How do I change my email address?**
A: Go to My Page → Profile Settings → Edit Email. You'll need to verify the new email address.

**Q: Can I delete my account?**
A: Yes, contact support to request account deletion. All data will be permanently removed.

**Q: How do I update my password?**
A: Go to My Page → Security Settings → Change Password. Enter current and new passwords.

#### Privacy & Security

**Q: Is my payment information secure?**
A: Yes, we use Stripe for payment processing. We never store your card information.

**Q: How is my personal data protected?**
A: We follow GDPR guidelines and use industry-standard encryption for all data.

**Q: Can I export my data?**
A: Yes, contact support to request a data export of your account information.

### 7.2 E-commerce Questions

#### Purchases & Refunds

**Q: What is your refund policy?**
A: We offer a 30-day money-back guarantee for all digital products.

**Q: How do I access my purchased products?**
A: You'll receive an email with download links immediately after purchase.

**Q: Can I transfer my purchase to someone else?**
A: No, purchases are non-transferable and tied to your account.

#### Payment Methods

**Q: What payment methods do you accept?**
A: We accept major credit cards, digital wallets, and bank transfers.

**Q: Do you offer payment plans?**
A: Currently, we only offer one-time payments for products.

**Q: Are there any hidden fees?**
A: No, the price shown is the final price. No additional fees.

### 7.3 Technical Questions

#### Platform Usage

**Q: Is the platform mobile-friendly?**
A: Yes, our platform is fully responsive and works on all devices.

**Q: Do I need to install any software?**
A: No, everything runs in your web browser. No downloads required.

**Q: Can I use the platform offline?**
A: No, an internet connection is required for all features.

#### Content Management

**Q: How often is content updated?**
A: New content is added regularly. Follow our blog for updates.

**Q: Can I suggest new features?**
A: Yes, we welcome feedback. Use the contact form to submit suggestions.

**Q: How do I report bugs?**
